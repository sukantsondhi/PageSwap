Rearrange Your PDF Pages & Images

Upload PDFs and images (JPG, PNG, HEIC, WebP), drag & drop to reorder, and export as a single PDF. 100% Local Processing - Your files never leave your device.

Why PageSwap?

In a world where online PDF tools often upload your sensitive documents to remote servers, PageSwap stands different. Your files are processed entirely in your browser - they never leave your device. This means complete privacy for your personal documents, contracts, medical records, or any sensitive information.

Why Free?

We believe everyone deserves access to essential document tools without paywalls or privacy compromises. PageSwap is and will always remain free because document management shouldn't be a luxury. If you find this tool helpful, consider buying me a coffee to support ongoing development.
